TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
=============================================================================

ISO 9660 Format:
- Primary Volume Descriptor at sector 16
- El Torito Boot Catalog at sector 17
- Boot Image at sector 19
- Total sectors: 100 (200 KB)

Boot Configuration:
- Media Type: 1.44 MB Floppy
- Load Segment: 0x0001
- Boot Entry: 0x88 (bootable, no emulation)
- Load Count: 1 sector (512 bytes)

RIFT Header (mmuko-os.img):
- Magic: "NXOB" (OBINexus)
- Version: 0x01
- Checksum: 0xFE
- Flags: 0x01
- Boot Signature: 0x55AA
